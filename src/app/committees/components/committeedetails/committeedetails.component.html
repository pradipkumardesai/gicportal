The purpose of Food Committee is to;

  • Listen to requirements/suggestions regarding food from teams.
  • Access the quality and quantity of food provided by vendor from time to time.
  • Make recommendations to Vendor.
  • Organize meetings with the vendor and give feedback.
  • Follow-up with vendor regarding suggestions and recommendations.
  • In case change in vendor is required, to handle the entire process from finding new vendor to getting vendor on board.
  • Manage internal communications regarding Food Committee updates/activities.
